Introduction

Background Story

The city is being hit by an infinite number of meteorites coming from outer space. The only way to save it is by typing the words on those meteorites and shoot them with stun grenades. Watch out for the mutant meteorite!

Controls - more than 36 keys

any key
Type the "alphanumeric" components

Game Mechanism

Win/ Loss
There's no such thing in this game. The only thing is keep going to higher level and earn more scores. If the ground of the city has shrunk to nothing, you lose T.T
Level Up
By destroying a number of meteorites, the level increases, meaning more lengthy words, and fulling the hp bar.
Destroy meteorite
Type all words on the meteorite while not making any mistake to destroy it. If any letter is mis-typed, the status will be cleared.
Destroy city
Word length = Attack point = Influence on the ground of city. Therefore, hit longer words before all :D.
